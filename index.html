How It Works

Inquiry

Share the occasion, preferred date, guest count, location, dietary needs, and any special theme—our team replies with a tailored proposal within 24 hours.

Menu Customization

Collaborate with our executive chef in a tasting session or virtual call. We’ll refine dishes around seasonality, spice level, budget, and presentation style until every bite is spot‑on.

Event Day

On your big day our crew arrives early, sets up kitchen stations and décor, cooks everything fresh on‑site, serves guests with warm Kenyan hospitality, and leaves the venue spotless.

Our Story

Ngara Cooks began as a humble start-up in the heart of Ngara, yet from day one our kitchen was built state-of-the-art—outfitted to extract the very best from every ingredient. We believe that when cutting-edge technique meets genuine care, even the simplest dish can feel extraordinary.

Today our team is bigger, but the mission is still the same: craft honest meals that feel like home while giving every guest a memorable experience. Whether it’s a backyard birthday or a 500-seat gala, we season each plate with the passion that sparked our earliest cook-outs—ensuring quality, warmth, and a little bit of Ngara pride in every bite.
